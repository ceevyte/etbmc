Escape The Backrooms In Minecraft

A Minecraft recreation of Escape The Backrooms from scratch.

About

ETBMC is a faithful-but-reimagined port of the Steam game Escape The Backrooms into Vanilla Minecraft. Built on Minecraft Java Edition 1.21.11 with no mods required* to play.
